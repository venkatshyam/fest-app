Registration process will start from 13th and will be open during all the time of fest except for 6pm to 11pm during the time of fest.Entry for fest will be allowed after the completion of regestration.

No,food is not included in registration fee. However there are food stallsavailable in the arena as long as it open. Apart from it there are varies eateries and canteens in the campus where food is available at affordable rates.
